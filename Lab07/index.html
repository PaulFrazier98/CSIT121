The Best Spaghetti Carbonara

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces (225 grams) spaghetti
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup (100 grams) grated Pecorino Romano cheese
  • 4 ounces (115 grams) guanciale or pancetta, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (for garnish)

Instructions:

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.

This is my all-time favorite recipe because it's a classic Italian dish that never fails to impress. The combination of creamy egg and cheese sauce with crispy guanciale and perfectly cooked pasta is simply irresistible!
